Our Process

Analysis

  • We match you with the right designer who will give you more information on our company and go over your project in detail.
  • We'll answer any questions you may have about your outdoor project.

Measure

  • We then schedule a time to measure your property.
  • We take photos and exact measurements to put into CAD to create your design and give you an accurate estimate.

Design

  • We'll first create a CAD plan to go over with you and discuss more of your ideas and present pricing options.
  • Then, we'll move forward with the full landscape and 3-D design.

Build

  • We then pull all permits needed for your project
  • Our specialty crews will demo, construct, and clean your new space-transforming your designs to reality.
